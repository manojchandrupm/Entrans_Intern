Artificial Intelligence (AI)
    └─ Machine Learning (ML)
           └─ Deep Learning (DL)
                 └─ Large Language Models (LLMs)
Natural Language Processing (NLP) often uses ML and DL for tasks

Artificial Intelligence:
 	- it is a computer based field focus on creating software and Machines , that will trying to act smart like humans.

Machine Learning :
 	- it is subset of AI , the machine will learn from data instead of programmed rules.
 	- which is used for prediction and decision making.

Deep Learning :
 	- it is a subset of machine learning where DL will have the artificial neural network to learn complex patterns from data.
 	- the complex data like speech, image and text.

Large Language Models:
 	- it is a type of Deep learning model were the model is trained on the large text data to understand and generate the human language.

Natural Language Processing:
 	- it is subset of Ai and used in ml and dl to understand and generate response in human language.
